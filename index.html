The 4-Phase Cycle

From creation to optimization to deployment to iteration

1

CREATION

Build a crystal-clear base prompt with structured XML tags: objective, context, requirements, steps, output, verification.

<objective>...</objective> <context>...</context> <requirements>...</requirements>
2

OPTIMIZATION

Elevate with frontier tactics: Chain of Thought, Tree of Thoughts, personas, self-critique loops, and advanced techniques.

  • Flaw audit (3-5 bullets)
  • Inject 2-3 amplifiers
  • Radically clear output
3

DEPLOYMENT

Model-tuned execution plan adapted for your target LLM (Grok, Claude, Gemini, etc.) with single, parallel, or sequential runs.

ChatGPT Claude Grok Gemini
4

ITERATION

Track progress with scoring, verification checklists, and cycle logs. Continuously improve through feedback loops.
