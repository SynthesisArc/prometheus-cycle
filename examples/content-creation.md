## üí° Content Creation Principles

### 1. Pattern Interrupt is Everything
The first 2 lines must stop the scroll. Use:
- Counterintuitive statements
- Vivid micro-stories
- Provocative questions
- Surprising statistics
- Bold declarations

### 2. Narrative Arc > Listicles
LinkedIn is saturated with "5 tips" and "3 lessons" posts. Stand out with:
- Story structure (setup, conflict, resolution)
- Tension and release
- Emotional journey
- Insight that lands

### 3. Engineer Quotability
One line should be so good people screenshot it. Characteristics:
- Stands alone without context
- Memorable phrasing
- Universal truth
- Slightly provocative

### 4. End with Genuine Curiosity
Not "What do you think?" but a specific, thoughtful question that invites real conversation.
